About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, founded in 1902, is one of the most successful and iconic football clubs in the world. Based in Madrid, Spain, the club is famous for its rich history, legendary players, and record-breaking achievements. Real Madrid represents excellence, dominance, and a winning mentality.

Santiago Bernabéu Stadium

The Santiago Bernabéu Stadium is the home of Real Madrid. With a seating capacity of over 80,000, it is one of the most famous football stadiums in the world and a symbol of the club's greatness.

Major Trophies

  • UEFA Champions League – 14 Titles
  • La Liga – 35 Titles
  • Copa del Rey – 20 Titles
  • FIFA Club World Cup – 5 Titles

Club Legends

Legends like Alfredo Di Stéfano, Cristiano Ronaldo, Raúl, Iker Casillas, and Zinedine Zidane have defined the club's legacy and inspired millions of fans worldwide.
